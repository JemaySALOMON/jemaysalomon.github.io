Research Interest

I am a Junior quantitative geneticist researcher focused on developing statistical and computational tools to dissect the genetic architecture of complex traits and to optimize plant breeding programs. My work sits at the intersection of mixed model theory, stochastic simulation, and tools development — translating methodological advances into practical, scalable breeding strategies.

My research interests span genetic effects and (co)variance component estimation, genomic selection, simulation-based optimization of multi-stage breeding schemes, the quantitative genetics of complex traits and using AI to accelerate the breeding of superior cultivars.
